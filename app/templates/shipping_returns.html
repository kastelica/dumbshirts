Shipping & Returns

Shipping

  • Free shipping (3–5 business days) on domestic orders.
  • Express shipping (2 business days) available at checkout.
  • Production time: 1–3 business days. Orders print on demand.
  • Tracking is emailed as soon as your order ships.

Returns

  • We accept returns for misprints or damaged items within 30 days.
  • Contact support with your order number and photos for a quick replacement.
  • Custom items are printed to order; size/color exchanges are not guaranteed.

Subscriptions

  • Monthly shirt subscription: $15.00 per month, billed every 1 month.
  • Free US shipping on every subscription shipment.
  • Recurring charge continues until canceled. Manage or cancel anytime.
  • No discounts or sales apply to subscription pricing.
  • Cancellations apply to future renewals; in‑process orders may ship.

Support

Email support at support@dumbshirts.store.
