Pricing, payment, shipping, and returns (Important)

We want pricing and purchase terms to be clear before checkout. Here is what you can expect when buying from Roast Cotton:

  • Total shown at checkout: The final amount you pay is shown before you place your order, including item price, shipping option (if any), and applicable sales tax.
  • No membership or hidden fees: We do not require a paid membership, subscription, or contract to buy standard products from our store.
  • Payment timing: Your payment method is charged when you place your order.
  • Shipping choices: Free shipping and express options are presented at checkout with estimated delivery windows.
  • Returns and refunds: Eligible returns are accepted within 30 days according to our policy, and approved refunds are issued to your original payment method.
